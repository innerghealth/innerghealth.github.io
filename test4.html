Meet Griselle Q.

Griselle has encountered life experiences and obstacles that led her to pursue a life abundant in health and wellness. She realized the power of integrated holistic medicine through first hand experience after having faced childhood traumas that affected her quality of life as a child and young adult. Disregarded as a major issue from her family, she believed this was a normal way of living. Dissatisfied, yet enduring over a decade of deep stress and anxiety, she sought therapeutic approaches to alleviate of her physical, mental and emotional pain. Her road to everlasting transformation includes holistic therapies such as chiropractic care, massage therapy, acupuncture, aromatherapy, and biodynamic craniosacral therapy in chronological order. Griselle received her massage therapy and Asian bodywork certification from Pacific College of Oriental Medicine in 2016 and soon began working alongside doctors of holistic medicine in the very same year. Griselle received her Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy certification through Body Intelligence in 2018. She is a member of the American Massage Therapy Association and The Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy Association of North America. With an appreciation of eastern and western healing modalities, Griselle combines techniques from both perspectives to deliver a customized massage based on her client's specific needs, preferences, and wellness goals. Griselle aims to provide an enjoyable, wholesome and nourishing experience. During her free time, Griselle enjoys cooking, swimming, practicing Seven Star Praying Mantis Martial Arts, and gaining knowledge in her field as a holistic and restorative care practitioner. Griselle currently runs her practice, in Lakeview, and works alongside Dr. Torres as his lead massage therapist at Seven Star Wellness.

Meet Dr. Torres

Yamel Torres is the founder and Sifu of the Chinese Martial Arts and Seven Star Wellness Center which opened its doors to the Chicago community in 1998. Prior to opening his school, Yamel was an Electrical Engineer and Software developer professional earning a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Chicago and a Master of Science in Computer Science from DePaul University. He later transitioned to a career in the healing arts and earned a Master of Science in Oriental Medicine from the Pacific College of Oriental Medicine, where he also served as a faculty member. Yamel deepened his knowledge and skills by earning a Doctorate of Acupuncture and Chinese Medicine in August of 2017. Dr. Torres runs his private practice, in Lakeview, providing Acupuncture and herbal medicine for the past 7 years. Dr. Torres also works with Dr. Gilberto Munoz at Chicago Medical Group providing consultation on Acupuncture. Additionally, Dr. Torres has studied Traditional Martial Arts since the age of 12 and has been teaching for over 22 years. When he is not seeing patients, Dr. Torres teaches at his school Traditional Martial Arts.
