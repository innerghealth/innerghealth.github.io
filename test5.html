Griselle has encountered life experiences and obstacles that led her to pursue a life abundant in health and wellness.

She realized the power of integrated holistic medicine through first hand experience after having faced childhood traumas that affected her quality of life as a child and young adult. Disregarded as a major issue from her family, she believed this was a normal way of living. Dissatisfied, yet enduring over a decade of deep stress and anxiety, she sought therapeutic approaches to alleviate of her physical, mental and emotional pain.

Her road to everlasting transformation includes holistic therapies such as chiropractic care, massage therapy, acupuncture, aromatherapy, and biodynamic craniosacral therapy in chronological order. Griselle received her massage therapy and Asian bodywork certification from Pacific College of Oriental Medicine in 2016 and soon began working alongside doctors of holistic medicine in the very same year. Griselle received her Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy certification through Body Intelligence in 2018.

She is a member of the American Massage Therapy Association and The Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy Association of North America. With an appreciation of eastern and western healing modalities, Griselle combines techniques from both perspectives to deliver a customized massage based on her client's specific needs, preferences, and wellness goals. Griselle aims to provide an enjoyable, wholesome and nourishing experience.

During her free time, Griselle enjoys cooking, swimming, practicing Seven Star Praying Mantis Martial Arts, and gaining knowledge in her field as a holistic and restorative care practitioner. Griselle currently runs her practice, in Lakeview, and works alongside Dr. Torres as his lead massage therapist at Seven Star Wellness.
